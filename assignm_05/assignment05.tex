\section*{Exercise 4}

\subsection*{Task}

For any $\gamma > 0$ the solution of $\min_{w, b} \frac{1}{2} \|w\|^2$, subject to $y_i \cdot (\langle w, x_i \rangle -b) \geq 1$ is the same as the same one subject to $y_i \cdot (\langle w, x_i \rangle - b) \geq \gamma$.

\subsection*{Answer}
\begin{align*}
  & y_i \cdot (\langle w, x_i \rangle - b) \geq \gamma \\
  \Leftrightarrow & \gamma^{-1} \cdot y_i \cdot (\langle w, x_i \rangle - b) \geq 1 \\
  \Leftrightarrow & \gamma^{-1} \cdot y_i \cdot (\langle w, x_i \rangle - b) \geq 1 \\
\end{align*}
