\section*{Exercise 1}
Write the following linear program in the standard
form by determining \textbf{A,b,c}.

Substitute $x_3$ with $x_3' = -x_3$.\\
minimize $x_1 -2x_2 -4x_3'$ \\
subject to\\
$-x_1+x_2 \geq 1$ \\
$3x1 - 2x_3' \leq -1$ \\
$-2x_1 + 5x_3' +4 \leq 0$\\
and\\
$x_1,x_2,x_3' \leq 0$\\ \\
\textbf{Standard form:}\\ \\
minimize $c^Tx$ \\
subject to $Ax \leq b$ \\
and $x \leq 0$ \\
with 
$
A=
\begin{bmatrix}
-1 & 1 & 0 \\
3 & 0 & -2 \\
-2 & 0 & 5 \\
\end{bmatrix}
b=
\begin{bmatrix}
1 \\
-1 \\
4 \\
\end{bmatrix}
c=
\begin{bmatrix}
1 \\
-2 \\
-4 \\
\end{bmatrix}
$
